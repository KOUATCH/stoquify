# Kontava Brand Naming Exercise

Date: 2026-06-19

## 1. Naming Strategy

The platform name should not sound like a small POS app, stock tool, or accounting plugin. It should sound like the operating backbone of a serious business.

The name should communicate five ideas:

1. Trust: accountants, owners, auditors, and banks must believe the numbers.
2. Control: owners must feel they can see cash, stock, staff, payroll, purchasing, and compliance in one place.
3. Intelligence: the platform should feel smarter than ordinary accounting software.
4. Growth: the name should work for a small shop, a multi-branch business, and eventually larger enterprises.
5. OHADA-readiness without being trapped by OHADA: the name should fit the OHADA zone, but still allow expansion into broader African SMB infrastructure.

The strongest naming direction is a short coined name plus a clear descriptor.

Example:

```text
Kontava
The trusted business operating system for OHADA SMBs.
```

This gives the product a memorable brand name while the descriptor explains the category.

## 7. Top 5 Strongest Names

### 1. Kontava

Best overall. It is short, professional, easy to pronounce in English and French contexts, and broad enough for accounting, POS, inventory, payroll, compliance, reconciliation, and finance. It sounds like a real SaaS company, not a feature.

Best positioning:

```text
Kontava
The trusted business operating system for OHADA SMBs.
```

Best tagline:

```text
Every operation, proved.
```

### 2. Nexada

Best modern and future-facing option. It subtly echoes OHADA without being too literal. It feels like a next-generation infrastructure product.

Best positioning:

```text
Nexada
The next operating layer for African SMBs.
```

Best tagline:

```text
Run the business. Trust the numbers.
```

### 3. Veridara

Best trust, compliance, and audit option. It sounds premium and serious. It is very strong if the product wants to lead with auditability, compliance, and financial truth.

Best positioning:

```text

A trusted operating platform for compliant business growth.
```

Best tagline:

```text
Business truth, from sale to ledger.
```

### 4. Corevia

Best enterprise infrastructure option. It communicates that the platform is the core of the business, not just a software tool.

Best positioning:

```text

The operating core for modern SMBs.
```

Best tagline:

```text
Connect the work. Control the business.
```

### 5. Ohadex

Best OHADA-specific option. It is clear, direct, and compliance-heavy. The weakness is that it may feel too regulatory and less flexible if the company expands beyond OHADA.

Best positioning:

```text
Ohadex
The business operating system built for OHADA compliance.
```

Best tagline:

```text
OHADA-ready from transaction to report.
```

## 8. Recommended Final Name

The strongest recommendation is:

```text
Kontava
```

Use the full market-facing identity as:

```text
Kontava Business OS
```

Or for formal positioning:

```text
Kontava: The trusted business operating system for OHADA SMBs.
```

Why this is the best choice:

1. It sounds modern and enterprise-grade without being cold.
2. It is broad enough to cover all modules: accounting, POS, inventory, payroll, purchasing, finance, reconciliation, compliance, and audit.
3. It does not trap the company inside only POS, only stock, or only accounting.
4. It works for owners, accountants, fintech partners, and regulators.
5. It can support a serious product family later: Kontava Ledger, Kontava POS, Kontava Payroll, Kontava Reconcile, Kontava Compliance, Kontava Partner Portal.
6. It has enough uniqueness to feel ownable, though it still needs formal trademark, domain, and registry checks.

## 13. Next Validation Steps

Before selecting the final public name, the team should validate:

1. Trademark availability in the target markets.
2. Company registry conflicts.
3. Domain availability for `.com`, regional domains, and practical alternatives.
4. Social handle availability.
5. French and English pronunciation.
6. Meaning or unintended associations in key OHADA-zone languages.
7. Customer reaction from owners, accountants, fintech partners, and regulators.

## 14. Final Recommendation

Move forward with `Kontava` as the preferred brand candidate, supported by `Nexada`, `Veridara`, `Corevia`, and `Ohadex` as backup candidates for legal and market validation.

Recommended launch expression:

```text
Kontava Business OS
Every operation, proved.
```
